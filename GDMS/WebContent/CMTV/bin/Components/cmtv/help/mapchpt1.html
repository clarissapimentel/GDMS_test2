1 Introduction

The Comparative Map and Trait Viewer (CMTV) is one of the ISYS components developed as part of the collaboration of the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR) and the Consultative Group of International Agricultural Research (CGIAR). Other tools developed as part of this collaboration are the ICIS Data Provider, the ISYS Matrix Viewer, and the ISYS Graph Viewer.
