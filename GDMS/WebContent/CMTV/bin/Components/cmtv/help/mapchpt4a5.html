Removing Map Data

Once map data are loaded, they remain available in the Viewer and are listed in the pane until you exit the Viewer or remove the data from it.

To remove map data from the pane

    • Select the map(s) you want removed

      Note: To select more than one map, press and hold <Control> as you select the maps. Or, to select contiguous maps, click and drag the mouse cursor over the maps or select the map at the beginning or end of the contiguous block, press and hold <Shift>, and select the map at the other end of the block.

    • Select Delete

The Delete Maps dialog appears.

    • Select Yes

The maps are removed from the list. Any graphical representations of them in the Map Viewer pane are also removed.
