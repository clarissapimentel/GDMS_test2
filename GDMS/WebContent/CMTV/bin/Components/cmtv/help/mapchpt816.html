Adjusting the Size of the Graph

A Zoom scale is provided at the bottom of the Graph Viewer window. When a graph is initially displayed, the Viewer attempts to show the entire graph on the screen. The slider/pointer on the Zoom scale is adjusted to show you the zoom level. You can move the slider to adjust the resolution of the graph. As you zoom in, scroll bars are added to the right and bottom of the graph area so you can move the view. The Size to Fit option lets you again see the entire graph in the window.

To zoom in/out

    • Click and drag the slider on the Zoom scale

      OR

    • Select the line to the left or right of the slider

The graph size is adjusted. You may find that you have a blank window. If so, you can use the scroll bars to find the graph. For help with this, see Using Scroll Bars.

To see the entire graph in the window

    • Select Size to Fit

The graph size is adjusted so that the entire graph appears in the window.
