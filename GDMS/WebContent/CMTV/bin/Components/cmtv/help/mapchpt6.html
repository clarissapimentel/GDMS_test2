6 Comparing Maps

The Viewer lets you compare two or more maps. When you do this, the Viewer generates a graphical representation of the comparison.

There are two ways to generate comparisons in the Viewer:

  • Letting the Viewer Find Comparable Maps
  • Having the Viewer Compare Selected Maps

You can choose from multiple comparison algorithms in the Viewer. You can also use your own comparison criteria. For more information about this, see Defining Your Own Comparison Service.
