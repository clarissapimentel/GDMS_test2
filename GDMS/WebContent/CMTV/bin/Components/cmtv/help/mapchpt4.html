4 Loading Map Data

In the Comparative Map Viewer, you can load and work with map data from various locations and sources and in various formats. When you choose to load map data, the maps are loaded into the Viewer and listed in the Maps Currently Available pane. You can select to generate graphic images and map comparisons of the maps listed in this pane. If the map data you load include comparisons, they are listed in the Map Comparisons pane. Once map data are loaded during a session, they are always listed unless you select to remove them.

Before you try to load map data make sure you know its format. You can select to load map data from various flatfile formats. Depending on your system, you may also be able to choose to load data from a database. The load options available in the Viewer depend on the components that are plugged into your ISYS system.
