Invoking DynamicDiscovery

In the Comparative Map Viewer you can access DynamicDiscovery from two areas.

    • Select one or more objects in a graphical map
    • Press the right mouse button while pointing to the map

      OR

    • Press the right mouse button while pointing to the desktop outside any map

The DynamicDiscovery menu appears.

This menu may look very different depending on the components in your ISYS system and where your mouse was pointing when you pressed the right mouse button. When you are pointing to a particular map and invoke DynamicDiscovery, the selected objects in that map only are used. When you are pointing to the desktop and invoke DynamicDiscovery, the selected objects in all maps are used.

Where you press the button will also make a difference in whether other components are synchronized with the Map Viewer. When you invoke DynamicDiscovery from a Map window, the map may be synchronized with other ISYS components you select. When you invoke from the desktop, there is no synchronization.
