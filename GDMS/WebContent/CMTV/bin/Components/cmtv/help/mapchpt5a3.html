Combining Map Data for Display

The Viewer lets you combine the data of two or more maps into a single graphical map. When you combine maps, all of the data from the maps are presented visually as if they are referring to a uniform coordinate system. Combined maps are only meaningful if you choose maps whose individual coordinate systems are more or less in agreement. Combined maps are virtual entities and do not get represented as a new map anywhere except the Map Viewer pane.

To view multiple maps as one map

    • Press and hold <Control> as you select the maps you want
    • Select View together

A single map appears in the Map Viewer pane, titled "Combined view".
