Comparing Maps

    • Press and hold <Control> as you select two or more maps in the Maps Currently Available pane

      OR

    • Press and hold <Control> as you select the title bars of two or more maps in the Viewer pane
    • Select Map comparisons >> Generate map comparison for selected maps

Lines are drawn between corresponding mapped objects in the compared maps and the comparison is listed in the lower left pane--the Map Comparisons pane.

For more information about comparing maps and the options associated with it, see Comparing Maps.
