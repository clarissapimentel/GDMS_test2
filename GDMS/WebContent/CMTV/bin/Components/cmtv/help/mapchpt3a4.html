Resizing Panes

When data are loaded you may need to adjust the size of the panes to see all that you want.

To resize panes

    • Move the mouse cursor to the "textured" line dividing the panes you want to resize
    • When the cursor becomes a line with an arrow pointing to it, click and drag the divider until the panes are the size you want
